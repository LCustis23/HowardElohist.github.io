Howard University  Elohist Club 

About 

Welcome to the Howard University Elohist Club. Our mission is to share the love of God to all students and testify about God Elohim-- God the Father and God the Mother. We are a Christian, non-denominational group with the soul focus of wanting to learn the TRUTH of the Bible. We Encourage all Howard Students to come learn this truth, and join us on the path to the Kingdom of Heaven.

Events 

The Howard University Elohist Club is comprised of 2 parts: IUBA and ASEZ. The goal of IUBA is to create a haven for students who are interested in True God to learn all the truths recorded in the bible. We create an environment where the members can stay close to the word of God in a daily life through media such as audio sermons, videos, websites, and books. ASEZ makes efforts to achieve the Sustainable Development Goals spearheaded by the United Nations, through worldwide environmental campaign. ASEZ will actively cope with climate change and environmental issues.
